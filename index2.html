The Beginning

an unexpected start

Beginings are always beautiful. Who knew that the begining would be so unexpected too. We never know what turn our lives can take at a certain point. Be it a path of discomfort and darkness or an aesthetic path of love and pleasure, we are bound to follow it. The almighty had decided to direct me to a path of love, care and affection. A teenager who never believed in the above mentioned theories, fell in the pit of love. Converging to the path of a girl like you was beyond my dreams. I still try to find that force which made me approach you. For such a shy teenager, approaching you was never a thought to ponder upon. Our story started with an uncertain fate. Both of us being shy, hesitant, sceptical and reserved. The doors of comfort and trust were visible after few months.

The Hurdles

the newbies

The first two years were tough. Restrictions, insecurities, privacy and limitations were the hurdles standing on our path. Being the newbies and immature teenagers, we found ourselves stuck between the barriers surrounding us. Mistakes were made from my side and I'm still apologetic for that. Few months of ignorance and unwanted distancing by me created a void in between. Seeing you and exchanging words had become my habit I could barely resist. The void had the essence of love. All the actions have a strong reason behind which we fail to see. Distancing from you had the one which made me realise your importance. The immature decision proved to be the reason behind the present maturity. I still laugh remembering those days of innocence and silliness. We have grown from the very beginning. We have faced diverse situations and held each other on every step of our unending stairs.

The Dilemma

prioritizing desires

The first decisive year of my career had arrived and you were the solid pillar backing me up. Our love was at a growing phase and we could see a brighter future together. Unfortunately my first career dream remained a dream for me. Despite all my efforts, I could not convert that dream into reality. Failure was the only word surrounding my mind. There was a pillar which did not let my strength collapse. Yes, you were the strongest pillar I could depend upon for solace. The year which made me fall for you with all my heart. The love deepened and I was assured that I could do the impossible with your presence in my life. The strength you helped me build has made me a man of rigidity and diligence. Today, if I have the courage to dream high, the credit goes to you, my love. Bringing me back from a dormant state and giving me reasons to live fearlessly, I owe you something that I can never pay you back. Your selfless love and care is beyond any pleasures of life. The smile your face carries is beyond all the wonders of the world. You are the only reason, I smile through all the hurdles in my life.

Parting Phase

a hefty gap

For the first time in 3 years, I understood what distancing from loved ones feels like. Missing someone was never a feeling I experienced. One complete year was a testing phase of our so called long distance relationship. Many unwanted thoughts crossed my mind. We fought, we cried, we made mistakes and we hurt each other, but the only constant was the 'love'. Loving each other was never kept aside. After all habits are not meant to be forgotten. A phase in relationship that often escalates negativity and results in a harsh ending. A year of mixture of emotions and misunderstandings often caught us in a locked room of uncertainities. The purity of love always finds a solution to overcome the most difficult situations and that was exactly what we experienced. Succeeding in the test of bond, we emerged stronger than ever. It was our love that came on top of all the disparities.

Sparkling Years

love saw no limits

Last 3 years have been the most important phase of our relationship. From one year of missing each other, to living in same city for next 3 years, the almighty rewarded us for our pure love for each other. Living the best time of my life in thse 3 years, I have loved to even more. We have taken mature decisions, we have cared for each other, we have fought, we have cried, but most importantly we have loved each other. The jokes we have cracked, the places we have been together, the laugh we have shared and the mistakes we have committed, all of these have contributes to our growth. The tree has been growing towards prosperity. We have lived to the fullest and expressed ourselves the most. I promise you that I'll fulfil every dream we have seen together. I promise you that I'll live up to the expectations you have from me. I promise you that I will keep loving you the way I have. I promise you that I'll be by your side whenever you will need me. I promise you that I'll give you all the happiness you deserve.

Cheers to 7 years of our relationship with a hope to continue till our last breath.

I love you sweetheart!
